ABOUT US

Light up the world with impact communication

Catholic sisters are cardinal in the mission of the church because they are involved in different pastoral works. They run projects in various spheres of life such as schools, hospitals, orphanages, social work, human trafficking etc.

Social Work

The Communication Network for Catholic sisters in Zambia is a tool of evangelization under the Zambia Association of sisterhoods (ZAS) that disseminate the activities and different works which the Catholic Sisters are involved in.

In partnership with Catholic Sisters Initiative of the Conrad N. Hilton Foundation, the communication network for catholic sisters seeks to provide the catholic sisters with the ability to create, write, compile and disseminate stories using different appropriate formats of social media.

The outcome of this project is to see that a number of sisters are able to tell their stories, showcase their impact and provide an advocacy platform on issues pertinent to Catholic sisters, integral human development, healthcare, education and other ministries.

Communication Network for Catholic Sisters is a community based organisation where the Catholic sister compose, narrate and depict their own stories.
